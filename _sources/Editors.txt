Text Editors
============

A programmers goto tool!!

Editors
-------

Here is a list of text editors we recommend you use. They all have support for GoLang and packages are able to be installed. There is no particular order.

- `VS Code`_
- `Atom.io`_
- `Sublime Text`_
- `Vim`_

Packages
--------

Here is a list of packages that may be useful for you!

.. note::
    These packages are not needed. They may help development.

.. note::
    These packages may not be available for your editor. Do some research and see if there is something similar for you.

- `VSCode-Go`_
- `Go-Plus`_ 
- `EditorConfig`_
